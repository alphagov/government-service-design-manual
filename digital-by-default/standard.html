The new standard for government services

All new or redesigned transactional services going live after
April 2014 must meet the new Digital by Default service standard.

A Digital by Default service is:

Agile

  • Owned by a single, suitably skilled service manager
  • Built and operated by a multidisciplinary team
  • Delivered using agile working practices
  • Tested and improved continuously
  • Has defined alpha and beta phases

Effective

  • Has high user satisfaction
  • Has a low cost per transaction
  • Has a high transaction success rates
  • Has high digital take-up

Open

  • Uses open platforms, standards and APIs where they exist
  • Establishes new ones where they don't
  • Has code and content that is reusable across government

Usable

  • Meets genuine user needs
  • Is used by everyone who can use digital services
  • Is accessible to those who can't use digital services
  • Is device and platform independent
  • Integrates seamlessly with the non-digital world
  • Feels like it's part of GOV.UK
